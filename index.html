STORIES OF TRANSFORMATION

GMA is continuing our work in Pakistan by raising funds to help provide food, medicine, and shelter in response to the historic flooding that Pakistan has been subjected to since June 14, 2022.
We partnered with our friend, the Grand Imam of Pakistan,Maulana Syed Muhammad Abdul Khabir Azad, to help deliver relief. One-third of the country is still underwater with over 1,650 lives lost and over half a million displaced. GMA was able to provide food for 566 families for an entire month!

CUBA // JESUS CHRIST KING OF THE NATIONS

JASSER RODRIQUES & AKI SERRANO

Just before opening our church, 8 years ago, the Lord told us to repair a medical clinic that belongs to the government of Cuba that is located in front of our church. So we went ahead and restored the clinic and added a beautiful garden. This act of love completely shifted the atmosphere in our community, which was full of witchcraft and darkness.
We were setting up an environment where the Kingdom could come and invade our city by honoring these people. As soon as we opened our church, the community readily accepted us and they were proud to call us their church. Heaven is invading Cuba, with joy instead of sadness, faith instead of fear, healing instead of pain, favor, provision... and LIGHT instead of darkness.
FATHER GOD, IN HIS GOODNESS AND KINDNESS, IS RAISING UP A FAMILY OF SONS AND DAUGHTERS THAT HAVE LEARNED HOW TO RECEIVE NATIONS.
